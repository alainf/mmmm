Determinants of Local
Governments' Transparency

The crisis has impact on Local Government
forcing it to be more transparent
in management of public resources.

This article examines theoretically and empirically the determinants of the levels of transparency in Local Governments based on the agency and legitimacy theories. For the purpose of this study, the analysis is based on the Spanish municipalities over a period of 4 years....
